ABOUT ME

About

PEOPLE PLACES

I'm a creator active in the fields of sound and visual art based in Madrid after a long stay in Berlin working in art management and engaging in many different artistic endeavorus.
People Places is my alter ego when making sound and art, while editions .pdf is a little publishing venture I initiated in 2019 in collaboration with California-based artist Rachel Simkover aka Book Hook.
Inspired by practices that defy and surpass the digital realm, my most recent project, Torneta Studio is also the most analogue; focused on the creation of one-of-a-kind hand-modeled pottery pieces.
